Bus Booking Application

The Elevator Pitch

Mallessa's Booking Online: is a digital solution designed to modernise public transport management. This application provides commuters with a seamless way to view routes, check availability, and reserve seats, transforming the stressful experience of bus travel into a structured, one-click process.

The Problem & Solution

  • The challenge: Traditional bus ticketing often involves long queues, manual errors, and a lack of real-time seat availability information.

  • The Solution: A centralized booking platform that allows passengers to browse specific travel routes and secure their transport ahead of time, ensuring a reliable commute.

Core Features

  • Route Discovery: An intuitive search system for users to find departures, arrivals, and specific travel times.

  • Station Management: Organized data display for various bus terminals and stops.

Technical Skills

  • Complex Data Display: Using HTML tables and grids to show bus schedules clearly.

  • Interactive Form Utilizing inputs like Bus Number, Selection of the Depature and destination
